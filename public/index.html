We want to help

We’ve created The Hassle Free Project to research the daily highs and lows of running a local independent business. We want to speak to business owners and operators running cafes, shops, community theatres, hairdressers, dry cleaners and more.

In return for your time we can offer some of our time for free. Put us to work behind the bar, cleaning up tables, or ask us for help with areas like your website, business strategy or digital marketing (that’s what we’ve done in the past).

We could help with:

  • Website design
  • Website optimisation
  • Emerging technologies
  • Business model tweaking
  • Business strategy
  • Cashflow forecasting
  • Finance options
  • Digital marketing
  • Social media campaigns
  • Print advertisement

Meet the team

We’re Evelina, Bailey and Tatseng and we're part of a social entrepreneurship program called Zinc. We’re hoping to learn more about how we might, in the future, build products and services to help support local independent businesses.

We’re working with local businesses across the UK, but particularly in Rochdale, working with Rochdale Development Agency and Rochdale Borough Council, and in Newcastle.

Why we care

We believe that independent businesses are at the heart of a vibrant local economy. We’ve had enough of cookie cutter high streets and want to see more shops, cafes and services in towns with their own unique identity. Run by local entrepreneurs, employing local talent.

As entrepreneurs ourselves, with backgrounds working in technology, we want to help build products and services to take as much hassle away from local business owners as possible so they can focus on serving their customers and not on the boring admin.
